14. Write a short note on Margins.

Definition: Margins are the blank spaces between the text and the edges of the page. They define the area where content can appear in a document.

Why are Margins Important?

  • Provide a clean and organized layout.
  • Ensure readability and proper alignment.
  • Allow space for binding or printing adjustments.

15. Write a short note on Orientation.

Definition: Orientation determines the direction in which a document is displayed or printed.

Types of Orientation:

  1. Portrait: The page is vertical (taller than wide).
  2. Landscape: The page is horizontal (wider than tall).

Why is Orientation Important?

  • Portrait: Ideal for text-heavy documents like letters or essays.
  • Landscape: Useful for wider content like tables, charts, or images.
